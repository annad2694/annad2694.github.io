Movie Webpage Project

Context

In my Software Engineering Class, the students had an objective to create a project. They were to create an original movie and use their knowledge of CSS and HTML to make a webpage to advertise said movie. My partner and I decided to continue the series of The Maze Runner by making a fourth and fifth movie. It is called "The Kill Order" and "The Fever Code" with similar characters but completely different stories.

Content

The world is in a haze from the global warming of the sun, and those who haven't already been killed by the heat are instead killed by a disease that spreads by contact. This apocalypse and climate issue have reached internationally. However, this group—the new generation, the ones immune to the disease and who can withstand the heat of the sun—adventure out to destroy the evil organization that had created all of this in the first place. From their previous journeys in the other movies, the group thinks they are finished with the organization WICKED once and for all, but little did they know that those they've left behind will make an appearance again; this time, they'll be on the opposing side, making WICKED possible once again.
